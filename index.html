Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: November 21, 2024

This document outlines how users can request the deletion of their personal data collected by PositivityHub.

How to Request Data Deletion

  1. Send an email to info@positivityhub with the subject line "Data Deletion Request."
  2. In your email, include the following information:
    • Your full name
    • Your email address associated with your account
    • A clear statement requesting the deletion of your personal data
  3. You will receive a confirmation email once your request has been processed.

Important Notes

Please note that once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. Ensure that you have saved any necessary information before submitting your request.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this process, please contact us at info@positivityhub.
